NP Membership Benefits

Our Non-Profit Membership is designed specifically for registered non-profit organizations.

  • Basic listing in online directory
  • Chamber newsletter subscription
  • Invitation to monthly networking events
  • Opportunity to collaborate with other non-profits
  • Access to community resources

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses and startups.

  • Standard listing in online directory
  • Chamber newsletter subscription
  • Invitation to monthly networking events
  • Social media mention (2x per year)
  • 5% discount on chamber events
  • Access to member-only resources

Annual Fee: $50

×

Silver Membership Benefits

Step up your involvement with our Silver Membership package, ideal for established businesses.

  • Enhanced listing in online directory with logo
  • Chamber newsletter subscription
  • Invitation to monthly networking events with 1 complimentary admission
  • Social media mentions (4x per year)
  • Business spotlight in newsletter (1x per year)
  • 15% discount on chamber events
  • Access to member-only resources and training
  • Participation in member committees

Annual Fee: $100

×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium Gold Membership provides maximum visibility and benefits for your business.

  • Premium listing in online directory with logo and extended description
  • Featured spot on Chamber home page (rotating basis)
  • Chamber newsletter subscription
  • Invitation to monthly networking events with 2 complimentary admissions
  • Social media mentions (monthly)
  • Business spotlight in newsletter (2x per year)
  • 30% discount on chamber events
  • Access to all member-only resources and premium training
  • Priority speaking opportunities at chamber events
  • Advertising opportunities in chamber publications
  • Direct referrals from chamber staff
  • Leadership position eligibility on chamber committees

Annual Fee: $250
