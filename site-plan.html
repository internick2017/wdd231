Site Plan - English Teaching Services

This document outlines the planning and structure for the English Teaching Services website project.

Site Name

LinguaLink Global

This name was selected because it effectively communicates the connection ("Link") between language ("Lingua") learning and global opportunity. The alliteration makes it memorable, and the name suggests a professional service with international reach, which aligns with the target audience of international students and professionals seeking English language instruction.

Optional domain availability: lingualinkglobal.com

Site Purpose

LinguaLink Global serves as a comprehensive platform for international students and professionals seeking to improve their English language skills. The site provides:

  • Detailed information about customized English learning services, including business English, conversational practice, and writing improvement
  • Real-time language learning resources pulled from external APIs
  • A secure client portal for accessing personalized learning materials and tracking progress
  • Downloadable practice materials in structured JSON format
  • Interactive assessment tools to help learners identify areas for improvement
  • Client testimonials and success stories to build credibility
  • Contact information and service booking functionality

The primary goal is to attract potential students by showcasing expertise and resources while providing existing students with valuable tools to enhance their learning experience.

Scenarios

  1. Business Professional Scenario: "I need to improve my business English for international meetings and presentations. What specific programs do you offer that can help me communicate more confidently in professional settings, and how quickly can I expect to see results?"

  2. University Student Scenario: "I'm preparing for the IELTS exam to study abroad. What resources do you provide to help me practice, and do you offer any personalized feedback on my writing and speaking skills?"

  3. Expatriate Scenario: "I've recently moved to an English-speaking country and need to improve my conversational skills for everyday situations. Do you offer flexible scheduling for lessons, and can I track my progress over time?"

Color Schema

The color scheme is designed to convey professionalism, trustworthiness, and engagement:

Primary
#2c6ea0
Secondary
#f5a623
Accent
#e64626
Background
#f8f9fa
Text
#333333
  • Primary Color (#2c6ea0): Deep blue conveys professionalism, trust, and knowledge. Used for headers, navigation, and key interface elements.
  • Secondary Color (#f5a623): Warm gold adds energy and positivity. Used for highlights, accents, and to draw attention to important elements.
  • Accent Color (#e64626): Bright accent for call-to-action buttons and important notifications to drive user engagement.
  • Background Color (#f8f9fa): Light gray creates a clean, professional backdrop that's easy on the eyes during extended reading.
  • Text Color (#333333): Dark gray for main text to ensure readability while being slightly softer than pure black.
